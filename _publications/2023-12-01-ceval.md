**C-Eval** is a large-scale, multi-level, multi-discipline evaluation benchmark for Chinese foundation models. It includes questions across 52 subjects (covering STEM, humanities, social sciences, and professional fields) at four difficulty levels (elementary, middle, high school, and college), enabling comprehensive assessment of model capabilities in Chinese language understanding, reasoning, and domain knowledge. The benchmark is widely used to evaluate and compare state-of-the-art Chinese and multilingual foundation models.
