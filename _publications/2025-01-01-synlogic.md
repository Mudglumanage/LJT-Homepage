This paper presents **SynLogic**, a method to synthesize large-scale, verifiable reasoning data for training and improving logical reasoning in large language models (LLMs). The approach addresses the challenge of limited high-quality reasoning data by automatically generating complex reasoning tasks with ground-truth solutions, enabling more robust logical reasoning capabilities. The method is validated across multiple logical reasoning benchmarks and shows strong performance improvements.
