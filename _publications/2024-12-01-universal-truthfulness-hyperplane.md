This paper discovers a **universal truthfulness hyperplane** hidden within the representations of large language models (LLMs). This hyperplane allows for the detection of untruthful (hallucinated or false) outputs, enabling effective mitigation strategies to improve model truthfulness. The finding is validated across multiple LLM families and tasks, providing a generalizable approach to enhance truthful generation.

**Co-authors**: Shiqi Chen, Yu Cheng, Junxian He
