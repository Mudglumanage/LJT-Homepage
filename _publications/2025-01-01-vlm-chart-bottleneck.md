This work explores the **perception bottleneck** in vision-language models (VLMs) for chart understanding. We identify key limitations in how VLMs process visual chart data, leading to suboptimal performance on chart-related reasoning tasks. The analysis provides insights into improving VLM capabilities for visual understanding of structured data like charts, with a focus on enhancing perception and reasoning over visual information.

**Co-authors**: Weihao Zeng, Xiwen Zhang, Yijun Wang, Zifei Shan, Junxian He
