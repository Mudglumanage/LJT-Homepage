This work investigates **composing parameter-efficient modules (PEMs)** using simple arithmetic operations to generate task-adapted models efficiently. PEMs (such as LoRA adapters) trained for specific tasks can be combined via addition, subtraction, or other operations to create new modules for unseen tasks, reducing the need to train new modules from scratch and enabling flexible adaptation of large language models (LLMs) to multiple tasks.

**Authors**: Jinghan Zhang, Shiqi Chen, Junteng Liu, Junxian He
